Hi there, I'm Neftali Loria.

For the past 15 years I've had the privilege to work on some incredible projects. One in particular enabled me to travel and capture the wonders of the world. Since then, my productions have inspired many creatives around the world.

Tech is moving fast...

A lot of people think that specializing into a creative career takes years of education. The truth is, we live in the most exciting times to become a designer, engineer or visual artist. The Problem

The problem is the lack of context and continuation most other courses provide. Typically they provide chunks of information but stop leaving you dry and cold without really understanding the entire process.

My Solution
