Follow the motion flow.

Join award-winning creative director Neftali in this Adobe Premiere course and learn to edit effective marketing videos to promote your own business, service or products.

Who Should Enroll?

This class is for designers, marketers, content creators, developers, and anyone looking to produce engaging Marketing Videos.

What will you learn? 

  • Pre-production workflows to discover the narrative & visual rhythm
  • Learn to edit & color engaging marketing videos in Adobe Premiere
  • You'll learn ways to design & create simple motion graphics
  • Prepare and export assets in different formats for social media
